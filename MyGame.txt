cAttributes
----------
Strength - +Melee Damage %
Stamina - +Health, +Energy
Agility - +Avoidance %, + 0,5 Dexterity
Dexterity - +Spell/Melee Multi Cast/Attack %, +Spell/Melee Damage %
Wisdom - +Resistance, + All stats 0,1;
Intelligence - +power, +Spell Damage %

Melee Attack Damage - Unlimited
Spell Cast Damage - Unlimited
Melee Multi Attack - (max-300%) = 3 attacks
Spell Multi Attack - (max-300%) = 3 attacks
